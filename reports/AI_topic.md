It has shown human-baseline performance on the OSWorld benchmark for operating system interaction. Sonnet 4.6 also shows significant improvement in resisting prompt injection attacks.
    *   **Claude Opus 4.6:** The company previously released Claude Opus 4.6, which is available for paid subscribers and represents a "fundamental shift" in handling complex workplace tasks.

*   **Google DeepMind:**

    *   **Gemini Embedding 2:** Google released Gemini Embedding 2, its first natively multimodal embedding model that can map text, images, video, and audio into a single embedding space. This model is available in public preview via the Gemini API and Vertex AI.
    *   **Genie 3:** This world model uses text descriptions to generate explorable photorealistic environments in real-time, representing a "new frontier for world models."

*   **Meta:**
    *   **"Avocado" Model Delayed:** Meta's next-generation foundational AI model, code-named "Avocado," has reportedly been delayed until at least May 2026. The delay is attributed to underperformance in internal tests related to reasoning, coding, and writing when compared to models from Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic. Meta has invested billions in AI development, with plans to spend up to $135 billion this year.
    *   **Licensing Considerations:** Due to the performance concerns with "Avocado," Meta is reportedly considering temporarily licensing Google's Gemini model to power its AI products.
