#  Topic: Liver and Kidney datasets

## What to Research

Search for the best sources of freely available liver and kidney datasets

- What are the currently best datasets in terms of
    - number of patients
    - number of scans
    - quality of scans
    - availability of annotations
    - extra clinincal data
- Look for patients with well described diseases, like cancers
- Look for non-restrictive licenses (prever cc-by over cc-by-nc or cc-by-md)
- Look for associated papers describing the datasets
- Highlight anything that's new since the last time we looked

## Report Format Preferences

Please organize the report with:
1. A brief executive summary (3-5 sentences)
2. Key announcements, grouped by organization
3. Notable papers with a one-sentence description each
4. A "What to Watch" section for upcoming releases or dates
5. Possible clinical relevance
