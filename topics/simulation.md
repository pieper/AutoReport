#  Topic: Simulation platforms

## What to Research

Search for any major developments in physics simulation technologies

- What are the currently best open source packages in terms of
    - modern architecture
    - accuracy of simulations
    - efficient implementation
    - high level programmability and customizability
    - active and highly competent community
- Look for simulations of human soft tissue, particularly for surgery
- Look for bioelectromagnetic simulations for TMS, TES, and PNS
- Look for non-restrictive licenses (prever cc-by over cc-by-nc or cc-by-md)
- Look for associated papers describing the work
- Highlight anything that's new since the last time we looked

## Report Format Preferences

Please organize the report with:
1. A brief executive summary (3-5 sentences)
2. Key announcements, grouped by organization
3. Notable papers with a one-sentence description each
4. A "What to Watch" section for upcoming releases or dates
5. Possible clinical relevance
