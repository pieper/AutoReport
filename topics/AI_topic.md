# Example Topic: AI Research Trends

## What to Research

Search for the latest developments in AI research over the past week. Focus on:

- New model releases or announcements from major labs (OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, Meta)
- Significant papers published on arXiv or in major conferences
- Benchmark results or capability improvements
- Any notable controversies or safety discussions

## Report Format Preferences

Please organize the report with:
1. A brief executive summary (3-5 sentences)
2. Key announcements, grouped by organization
3. Notable papers with a one-sentence description each
4. A "What to Watch" section for upcoming releases or dates
